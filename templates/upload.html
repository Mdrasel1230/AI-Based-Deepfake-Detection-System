About the Detection

Our system uses advanced AI models to detect potential deepfake manipulations in images and videos.

The detection process includes:

  • Face detection and extraction
  • Deep learning analysis for manipulation artifacts
  • Confidence score calculation
  • Visual explanation of the detected areas

After processing, you'll receive a detailed report with the detection results and visual explanations.

Detection Tips

To improve detection accuracy:

  • Upload high-resolution images or videos when possible
  • For videos, ensure they have a reasonable frame rate and duration
  • Images with clear facial features tend to yield more accurate results
  • Avoid heavily filtered or edited content that may interfere with detection
